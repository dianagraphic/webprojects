Sweet Toothers

Homemade Key Lime Pie

description

Ingredients

  • ghraham cracker crumbs - 2 cups (200 g, about 14 full graham crackers)
  • light brown sugar
  • melted butter - ½ cup (113 g)
  • salt
  • sweetened condensed milk - 14 ounces
  • Key Lime juice - ½ cup (114 g)
  • Key Lime zest - 4 teaspoons
  • eggs - 4
  • chilled heavy cream - 1½ cups (360 ml)
  • powdered sugar - ½ cup (57 g)
  • vanilla extract - ½ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Carefully separate the egg yolk from the egg whites and set the whites to the side (they will be used later) and pour the yolks into a bowl along with the zest and wisk together.
